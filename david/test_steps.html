Citizenship test

Rescheduling the test

Find out what to do if you didn’t take the test at all during the 30-day period in your invitation.

On this page

  • Online test
  • In-person or Microsoft Teams test
  • Urgent processing for emergencies

Online test

You missed your first test period

If this is the first time you missed the test, we’ll automatically invite you to the test again and send you a new invitation. You still have 3 chances to do the test within the new 30-day period.

You missed a second test period

If you missed a second test period, contact the email in the “Rescheduling” section of your test invitation and provide:

  • A detailed explanation of why you need to reschedule
  • Your application number
  • Your unique client identifier (UCI)

You missed a third test period

If you missed the test twice and didn’t contact us, we’ll abandon (stop processing) your application.

In-person or Microsoft Teams test

You missed your first test

If this was the first time we invited you to the test and you missed it, you have 2 options:

  • Contact us
  • Wait for a new invitation

Option 1: Contact us

Send us an explanation within 30 days of your missed test date:

  • Through our web form, or
  • By mail
Sending your explanation by mail

You can send your explanation by mail to the office that scheduled your test. Check your test invitation for the address of the office.

We’ll use the postmark date on your envelope as the date you sent your explanation.

After you contact us, we’ll send you a new invitation:

  • If your reason is valid, the new one will count as your first invitation.
  • If your reason is invalid, the new one will count as your final invitation.

Option 2: Wait for us to reschedule you

We’ll automatically reschedule your first test if:

  • You didn’t contact us to explain why you missed it previously, or
  • You contact us, but your reason for missing the test is invalid

We’ll send you a final invitation with the rescheduled date (usually 2 weeks after the date of your missed test). We may stop processing your application if you miss it again.

You missed a retest

If you missed your retest, you need to contact us (option 1).

If you contact us and give a valid reason for missing the retest, we’ll send you a final invitation. We may stop processing your application if you miss it again.

If you still want to be a Canadian citizen after we stop processing your application, you must re-apply and pay the fees again.

Urgent processing for emergencies

If you need to take the test sooner than the 30-day period on your invitation due to an emergency, check if you qualify for urgent processing.
