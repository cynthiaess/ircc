Answer: You need an electronic travel authorization (eTA)

You need an eTA to travel to or travel through (transit) Canada by air.

We approve most eTAs within minutes (by email). However, some applications can take several days if we ask you to submit supporting documents. Make sure you give yourself time to apply for and get your eTA.

Valid for:

  • Per visit: up to 6 months
  • In total: 5 years (or until your passport expires)

Cost: CAN $7

When you need it: to board your flight to Canada (you donâ€™t need it for flights within Canada or to leave Canada)

You also need: your passport or travel document (valid for your stay in Canada)
