Accounts for applications

IRCC Portal

Use this account to:

  • complete, update or view some applications
  • download electronic citizenship certificates
View services in the IRCC Portal
  • apply for a visitor visa, transit visa or super visa
  • apply for a study permit
  • submit an asylum claim
  • download an electronic citizenship certificate (if your citizenship application is approved and you chose an e-certificate)

IRCC secure account

Use this account to:

  • complete, update or view some applications
  • track some paper and online applications
View services in the IRCC secure account
  • apply for a visitor visa, work permit or study permit
  • apply for a visitor record and to extend work permits and study permits
  • apply to restore your temporary status
  • apply for Express Entry
  • apply for the Interim Federal Health Program (for refugees)
  • apply for a citizenship certificate
  • apply to search citizenship records
  • link paper applications to check your application status
  • check the status of some online applications:
    • Agri-Food Pilot
    • Atlantic Immigration Program
    • caregivers
    • citizenship certificates
    • Economic Mobility Pathways Pilot
    • family sponsorship
    • health-care workers permanent residence pathway
    • humanitarian and compassionate cases
    • out-of-status construction workers in the Greater Toronto Area
    • protected persons
    • Quebec-selected skilled workers
    • Quebec investors
    • Quebec entrepreneurs
    • Rural and Northern Immigration Pilot
    • search of citizenship records
    • self-employed persons
    • Start-up Visas
    • study permits
    • temporary resident permit holders
    • visitor visas, transit visas and super visas
    • work permits

You should also check our tool to confirm this is how to track applications for your situation.

Online citizenship application

Use this account to:

  • complete, update or view your application to become a Canadian citizen (citizenship grant)
  • calculate your physical presence (online applications only)

Permanent residence portal

Use this account to:

  • complete, update or view most permanent residence applications
  • apply for permanent resident (PR) cards
View services in the Permanent residence portal
  • apply for these permanent residence programs:
    • Agri-Food Pilot
    • Atlantic Immigration Program
    • Economic Mobility Pathways Pilot
    • Family sponsorship (spouse, partner, dependent children, parents, grandparents, and other relatives)
    • Home Child Care and Home Support Worker Pilots
    • Humanitarian and compassionate reasons
    • Permanent residence pathways for Hong Kong residents
    • Protected persons
    • Provincial Nominee Program (non-Express Entry process)
    • Quebec entrepreneurs
    • Quebec investors
    • Quebec self-employed persons
    • Quebec skilled workers
    • Rural and Northern Immigration Pilot
    • Self-employed persons
    • Start-up Visa
  • apply to renew or replace your permanent resident (PR) card
  • apply for your permanent resident travel document (PRTD)
  • confirm your presence in Canada

TR-to-PR portal

Use this account to:

  • view your application for the temporary resident to permanent resident pathway

Accounts to check application status

Application status tracker

Use this account to:

  • track Express Entry and some family sponsorship and temporary residence applications
View services in the application status tracker
  • track these applications:
    • Express Entry
    • family sponsorship (spouse, partner or dependent child applications only)
    • study permits
    • work permits
    • visitor visas

Citizenship application tracker

Use this account to:

  • track some Canadian citizenship applications
View services in the citizenship application tracker
  • track applications for Canadian citizenship

You can’t use this account to track citizenship applications for

  • members of the Canadian Armed Forces
  • people being adopted (through the citizenship process)
  • a citizenship certificate
  • searching citizenship records
  • giving up Canadian citizenship
  • resuming Canadian citizenship after giving it up

Find out how to check the status of these applications.
