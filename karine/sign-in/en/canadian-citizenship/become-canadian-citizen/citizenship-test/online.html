About the test

The online citizenship test

  • is in English or French
  • is 30 minutes long
  • has 20 questions that are either
    • multiple choice, or
    • true or false

You need to get at least 15 out of 20 questions right to pass the test.

For more on what to expect, read

  • a tutorial for the online test
  • about the citizenship test

Wait for your test invitation

You can only take the test after you receive our invitation.

You must complete the test within 21 calendar days of the start date in your invitation.

If you need to reschedule

Contact the email address in the Rescheduling section of your test invitation. In your email, include

  • a detailed explanation of why you need to reschedule
  • your application number
  • your unique client identifier (UCI)
If you’re outside of Canada

If you get your invitation and plan to take the test while you’re outside Canada, send an email to the address in the Eligibility section of your invitation.

In your email

  • put “Outside Canada – Citizenship Test” in the subject line
  • in the body of your message, include
    • your full name
    • your application number
    • the start and end date for your test

We’ll contact you with your next steps after we receive your email.

21-day period of the test

 You can take the test at any time during the 21-day period, including weekends and holidays, if you wish.

 You cannot sign in to the test

  • outside this 21-day period
  • before you receive the invitation, or
  • after you complete the test

 If the period between the “start date” and “end date” in your invitation is not 21 days, contact IRCC.CitOnlineTest-TestenligneCit.IRCC@cic.gc.ca with

  • a detailed explanation of the issue
  • a screenshot or attachment of the test invitation (the PDF in your invitation)
  • your application number(opens in a new tab)
  • your unique client identifier (UCI)(opens in a new tab)

You don’t need to contact us to get an invitation. We’re working as quickly as we can to invite eligible applicants to take the test.

If you gave an email address in your application, we’ll send you or your representative an invitation by email.

  • The test invitation letter will be a PDF file attached to the invitation email.
  • To avoid missing our emails, check your junk or spam folder.
  • Look for an invitation from an email address that ends in “@cic.gc.ca.”

If you or your representative did not give an email address,

  • you can give one by using our Webform, or
  • we’ll call you (check your voicemail messages regularly)

What you need for the test

You need

  • a piece of identification (ID), like
    • your permanent resident card (even if it’s expired), or
    • a valid (not expired) ID with your photo and signature on it (for example, driver’s licence or health card)
  • your application number(opens in a new tab) and unique client identifier (UCI)(opens in a new tab)
    • You can find them in your test invitation.
  • a valid email address
  • a reliable Internet connection
  • a desktop computer, laptop or tablet with a webcam
  • a Chrome or Safari Web browser
  • to close all other applications on your device
    • This includes applications that use your webcam or allow screen-sharing.
  • to take the test by yourself
  • to be in a quiet room with good lighting so our system can see you clearly through the webcam

Do not use the following to take the test:

  • a mobile phone
  • Chrome on an iPad
  • Microsoft Surface Pro
  • a virtual private network (VPN)

You may take the test in a library or with a public computer.

How our system will use your webcam

We’ll use your webcam to

  • take a photo of your face and photo ID
  • monitor you while taking the test

This process will confirm your identity and make sure you complete the test by yourself.

If you have a face covering and cannot remove it

  • email the office that sent you the test invitation (the email address is in the “Accommodations” section of your invitation)
  • ask the office for an accommodation
