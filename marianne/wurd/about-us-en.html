Web UX Research and Design
About the team

Who we are

We’re a small (but mighty) team of designers and researchers passionate about improving the online experience for IRCC clients. We are within the Web directorate in the Communications sector.

What we do

We strive to improve IRCC’s web presence by addressing user pain points using an evidence-based approach.

We focus on the non-authenticated space, including IRCC theme and topic pages, how to apply pages, and interactive tools.

We prioritize work that will have the largest impact on users and task success.

Our services

We’re happy to collaborate with you in the way that best suits your needs. Timelines on the following deliverables will depend on the scope and urgency of your request.

Examples of work we can deliver include:

  • Research reports, which typically include:
    • Quantitative data analysis (web and search analytics)
    • Qualitative data analysis (social networks, immigration forums, Task success survey, Page feedback tool)
    • Expert review based on usability heuristics
    • Design recommendations
  • Testing
    • A/B tests
    • Card sorts (open, closed, hybrid)
    • First-click tests
    • Tree tests
    • Surveys
  • Prototypes
    • Fully functional prototypes to bring your design ideas to life
  • Consulting and guidance
    • Recommendations on content design, research methods, usability best practices and more
