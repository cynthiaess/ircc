About the team

Who we are

We’re a small (but mighty) team of designers in the Web directorate, under the Communications sector.

We improve the online experience for IRCC clients by addressing their pain points, using an evidence-based approach. Our focus is the non-authenticated space, which includes the IRCC theme and topic pages, program content and interactive tools. We prioritize work with the largest impact on clients and task success.

Our services

We collaborate with you in the way that best suits your needs. Timelines for each project depend on the scope and urgency of your request.

Examples of the work we do include:

  • Research reports, which typically include:
    • Quantitative data analysis (web and search analytics)
    • Qualitative data analysis (social networks, immigration forums, Task success survey, Page feedback tool)
    • Expert review based on usability heuristics
    • Design recommendations
  • Testing and surveys
    • A/B tests
    • Card sorts (open, closed, or hybrid)
    • First-click tests
    • Tree tests
    • Surveys (standardized or non-standardized)
  • Content rewrites and page redesigns
    • Streamlining web content and optimizing the design elements of a page
  • Prototypes
    • Fully-functional prototypes to bring evidence-based design ideas to life
  • Consulting and guidance
    • Recommendations on content design, usability best practices, research methods, and more
