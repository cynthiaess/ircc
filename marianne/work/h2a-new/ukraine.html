Open work permit for Ukrainian nationals

Open work permits for Ukrainian temporary residents who are in Canada under the Canada-Ukraine authorization for emergency travel (CUAET) measures.

On this page

  • Who can apply
  • Get your document checklist
  • Fill out the application form

Who can apply

You may be eligible for an open work permit under the Ukraine immigration measures if you meet both of the following requirements:

  • Your CUAET application was approved on or after February 4, 2024.
  • You’re currently in Canada with valid temporary resident status under the CUAET measures.

If you’re eligible, you:

  • can apply to get a new open work permit that’s valid for up to 3 years

    The duration of your work permit

    The length of your work permit is directly tied to the expiry of

    • the passport (or other travel document) that you used to come to Canada
    • your biometrics

    They must both be valid for the entire time you’re in Canada.

  • can apply to extend your current open work permit
  • are exempt from paying the
    • work permit fee
    • open work permit holder fee
    • biometrics fee (if applicable)

Question Answer
What would you like to do in Canada? Work
How long are you planning to stay in Canada? Temporarily - more than 6 months
What is your current immigration status in Canada? Worker
Does one of the following apply to you?
  • I am applying for an open work permit under an active public policy or pilot program announced by IRCC
 Yes
There are fees associated with this application. Will you be paying your fees or are you fee exempt? No, I am exempt from paying fees for the application
Have you had a medical exam performed by an IRCC authorized panel physician (doctor) within the last 12 months? Yes

Upload your documents

Required document Where to upload the file
A letter stating "I am exempt from paying fees under the Ukraine special measures" Proof of fee exemption
Copy of Ukrainian passport or document used to travel to Canada/Identity document Passport
Digital photo Digital photo
Family Information Form (IMM 5707) Family Information Form (IMM 5707)

Job title UKRAINE 2022
Brief description of duties Ukraine 2022 public policy—open work permit
Start date Enter the date you’d like to start working. It may not be possible to finalize your application by the start date you ask for.
