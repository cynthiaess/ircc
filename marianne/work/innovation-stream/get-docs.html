Answer the following questions exactly as shown based on whether you’re applying from inside or outside Canada.

Upload your documents

If you used the Come to Canada tool to get your document checklist, sign in to your account to upload your documents.

You'll need a scanner or camera to create electronic copies of your documents.

Invitation letter

If you’re applying from inside Canada, you’ll need to upload a blank document in the “Invitation Letter” field of the application.

Outside Canada applicants don’t have an “Invitation Letter” field in their application.

Client information

There’s only one Client Information field with a maximum file size of 4 MB.

  1. Reduce the file size of each document before you save it.
  2. Combine your documents as one scanned file.
  3. Save the combined document as "Client information".
