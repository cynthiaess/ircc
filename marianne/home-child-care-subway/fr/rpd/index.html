Home Child Care Provider Pilot and Home Support Worker Pilot: Direct to permanent residence category

If you already have 12 months of qualifying work experience, you and your family members may be eligible to apply for permanent residence through the Home Child Care Provider Pilot or the Home Support Worker Pilot.

Processing times

29 months

Fees

Starting from: $CAN 1,085
