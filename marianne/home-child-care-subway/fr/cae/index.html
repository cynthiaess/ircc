Home Child Care Provider Pilot and Home Support Worker Pilot: Gaining experience category

You can apply to the Gaining experience category through the Home Child Care Provider Pilot and Home Support Worker Pilot if

  • you've never worked full-time as a caregiver in Canada, or
  • you have less than 12 months of work experience.

You'll submit a work permit application with your permanent residence application.

Processing times

33 to 35 months

Fees

Starting from: $CAN 1,085

Status

Home Child Care Provider Pilot:

  • Online Cap reached - Reopens Jan 2024
  • Alternate format Cap reached - Reopens Jan 2024

Home Support Worker Pilot Open
