Visitor visa

IRCC Portal: Option 1 of 2

You may have used the IRCC Portal to apply for a visitor visa:

  • to visit as a tourist
  • to visit for business
  • to visit family
  • for compassionate reasons
Go to IRCC Portal

IRCC secure account: Option 2 of 2

You may have used the IRCC secure account to apply for a visitor visa:

  • to visit as a tourist
  • for any other reason (that is not for business, family or compassionate reasons)
Go to IRCC secure account

If you’re not sure which account you used to apply, try signing in to both or check your email.
