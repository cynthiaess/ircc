We're still developing, improving and expanding what is available in this account.

Who can use this account

You may be able to use the IRCC Portal – New version if you meet all of the following requirements:

  1. You’re applying online for a visitor visa or super visa.
  2. You’re 18 years of age or older.
  3. You have never applied for anything with us before.
  4. You’re applying alone.
  5. You aren’t using a representative (including family or friends applying for you).
If you don’t meet all these requirements, review your program content to find out how you should apply:
  • Visit Canada
  • Immigrate to Canada
  • Work in Canada
  • Study in Canada
  • Canadian citizenship
  • Refugees and asylum
  • Permanent resident cards and travel documents
  • Canadian passports and travel documents
  • Adoption

You may have difficulty if you’re using assistive technology or devices

If you’re using assistive technologies or keyboard navigation, you may have difficulty tabbing to interactive elements only. You may notice that tabbing includes stops at non-interactive elements. For now, continue tabbing until you reach the interactive element you need. You should still be able to submit an application.
