Renew your passport online using the IRCC Portal – New version

We’re gradually rolling out online passport renewal.

Who can renew their passport online

  • Your current passport is a regular (blue) passport.
  • Your current passport has been valid for 5 or 10 years.
  • Your current passport expires within the next 6 months or has been expired for less than 1 year.
  • You applied for your current passport when you were 16 years of age or older.
  • Your current passport shows your place of birth.
  • Your current passport has more than 1 name listed on it (it doesn't have only a first or last name on it).
  • You did not declare your current passport lost, stolen, seized or surrendered.
  • You don't need your new passport for 25 business days (processing plus mailing time).
  • Your new passport will be mailed to a Canadian address.
  • You will use the same name, date of birth, place of birth and gender identifier on your new passport.

You need to get a digital passport photo. We’ve contacted photographers to let them know about this, but you may need to give them the specifications to be sure.
