You may be able to renew online if you need your passport in 20 business days or more. New accounts are limited and will be available until we reach our capacity for the day.

A Canadian passport is a travel document that Canadian citizens can apply for.

If you already have an adult passport, you may be able to renew it. Renewing a passport is simpler than applying for a new one. This is because you don’t need a guarantor, proof of Canadian citizenship or supporting identification.

From $120
