Traffic to IRCC sites remains stable compared to last month

Search engines (mainly Google) continue to drive most traffic to IRCC content, highlighting the importance of SEO (search engine optimization).

Of the traffic coming from social networks, over half of traffic is referred from Facebook.

  • Signing in remains a top task, as 1 in 5 visits includes visiting the secure sign-in landing page.
  • Users want to manage their applications. Of the topics on the Immigration theme page, over half of the clicks are on the My application link.
  • Visitor visa is clearly the most popular Visit program receiving nearly 3/4 of the doormat clicks.
  • Express Entry remains the most popular immigration program, receiving 1/3 of the doormat clicks on the Immigrate topic page.
  • Unsurprisingly, nearly half of doormat clicks on the Get a work permit page are for regular work permits for temporary workers.

Overall scores for task success, ease, and satisfaction have decreased very slightly, but remain stable.

Check processing times and Apply for an eTA continue to be top-performing tasks with task success scores above 80%.

Get, renew or replace a PR cardcontinues to be the worst-performing task with only half of users able to complete their task.

Contact us

The Passport Help Centre and the Client Support Centre services pages continue to drive the most clicks on the Contact Us link in the footer.

Notes:

  • Pages with less than 1K visits in a month are excluded.
  • Main "Contact IRCC" page is excluded.

Help centre

The Minister's message joins the Contact IRCC pages as the top pages with the most clicks on the Help Centre link in the footer.

Notes:

  • Help Centre link in footer is contextualized.
  • Pages with less than 1K visits in a month are excluded.
  • Help Centre content is excluded.

The news release about caregiver pilot programs becoming permanent was by far the most visited news release, representing almost 70% of traffic to all news releases.

The most popular special measures content continues to be the immigration measures for Ukraine.

2 of the top 5 campaign pages were related to eTAs for Mexican citizens, and another 2 from the top 5 were related to Francophone immigration.

Campaign pages for Before you apply and Claiming asylum continue to be top mobile pages and are joined by the news release on PGWP "flagpoling".

Note: Pages with less than 1K visits in a month are excluded.
